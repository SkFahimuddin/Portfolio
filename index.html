Artificial Intelligence


Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the development of computer systems capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as understanding language, recognizing patterns, solving problems, and making decisions. AI systems learn from data and experience, improving their performance over time without being explicitly programmed for each task.

Web Developer


Web development involves creating and maintaining websites, encompassing everything from designing the layout and user interface to coding the site's functionality. It includes front-end development (user-facing design and interactivity), back-end development (server-side logic, databases), and full-stack development (both front-end and back-end), ensuring a seamless online experience.

UI/UX


UI (User Interface) is the visual design of a product, focusing on how elements like buttons, icons, and menus look and interact. UX (User Experience) emphasizes the user's overall interaction with the product, aiming to make it intuitive, efficient, and enjoyable by optimizing functionality and ease of use.

My Projects

OTTO ROBOT


Otto is an open-source, 3D-printed bipedal robot designed for educational purposes and DIY robotics enthusiasts. Powered by an Arduino microcontroller and servo motors, it can walk, dance, and avoid obstacles using ultrasonic sensors. Otto is highly customizable, allowing users to add features like Bluetooth control, voice recognition, or additional sensors. It can be programmed using the Arduino IDE or visual platforms like Scratch, making it suitable for all skill levels. Otto’s modular design and strong global community support make it an excellent tool for learning robotics, coding, and engineering in an engaging, hands-on way

JARVIS


Jarvis is an AI-powered virtual assistant built using Python, designed to help users with a wide range of tasks through voice interaction. Equipped with speech recognition and text-to-speech capabilities, Jarvis can listen to commands, respond verbally, and perform tasks such as web searches, setting reminders, sending emails, or controlling smart home devices. Jarvis is highly customizable, allowing for the integration of additional features like natural language processing (NLP) for better understanding of user queries and automation scripts to streamline daily tasks. Its goal is to enhance productivity and simplify routine activities through an intuitive, voice-controlled interface.

PAGEPALLETE


Pagepalette is a user-friendly web app designed to help book lovers discover new reads by recommending books based on specific topics or authors. Users can easily search for their interests, entering keywords related to genres, themes, or names of authors. The app quickly retrieves a curated list of books that align with the user’s input, making it simple to find exciting new titles. With an intuitive interface and a comprehensive database, Pagepalette enhances the reading experience, encouraging users to explore diverse literary options and expand their personal libraries. It's the perfect companion for anyone looking to delve deeper into the world of books.

FITTECH


FitTech is a personalized fitness app designed to help users achieve their health and wellness goals by providing tailored diet recommendations and exercise guidance. By inputting their weight, height, and preferred sports or exercises, users receive customized diet videos that offer meal ideas and nutrition tips specifically aligned with their fitness needs. The app also features detailed descriptions of various exercises and sports, ensuring users understand proper techniques and benefits. With a focus on individual requirements, FitTech aims to create a holistic approach to fitness, empowering users to make informed decisions about their diet and physical activities for optimal results.
